Cauliflower Fried Rice Recipe

Description

This is the recipe of very delicious and healthy cauliflower recipe

A vegetable as cauliflower is a very good vegtable and combined with other recipe and fried rice it is the best

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 large clove garlic, crushed
  • ½ teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 (12 ounce) package Green Giant® Riced Cauliflower Medley
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten and scrambled
  1. step 1

    2. Heat vegetable oil in a large nonstick skillet and cook garlic and ginger over medium heat 1 minute

  2. step 2

    3. Add Green Giant Riced® Cauliflower Medley and cook 5 minutes, stirring frequently, or until vegetables are until tender-crisp.

  3. step 4

    4. Stir in cooked egg and continue cooking 1 minute or until heated through
